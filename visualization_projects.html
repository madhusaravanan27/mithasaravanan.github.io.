A Visual Exploration of Natural Disasters and Economic Impact in the USA (2000 - 2020)

This project presents a comprehensive visual analysis of natural disasters in the USA from 2000 to 2020, focusing on their frequency, human impact, and economic consequences. Using Tableau and data from FEMA and Our World in Data, it features interactive dashboards that reveal trends in disaster occurrences, recovery times, and financial damage across regions. These insights aid in disaster preparedness, recovery strategies, and policy planning.
